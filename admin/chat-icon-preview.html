🐦 Songbird Chat Icon — Variants

Actual 56px button size, on your site's background tone

A. Perched Silhouette

Dark bird on the button, gentle bob, wing flap, notes drift up. Elegant, restrained.

B. Round Cartoon

Plump, chubby bird with a blinking eye and flapping wing. Warm and friendly.

C. Tweety-Style

Bright yellow, cheerful, hops rhythmically. Playful — might feel too cartoonish for hymns.

D. Line-Art Minimal

Thin line silhouette, very minimal. Refined, reads more as a logo than a mascot.

F. Bird on Songbook

Bird perched on an open hymnal, head tilts up as it "sings." Strong hymn tie-in.

G. Duet (two birds)

Two birds alternating "chirps" like call-and-response. Evokes choir harmony.

H. Bird on Treble Clef

Bird perched on a stylized treble clef. Elegant, musical, logo-like.

I. Dove with Note

White dove carrying a music note. Traditional Christian symbolism, warm and reverent.

J. Bird in Speech Bubble

Chat-bubble outline with bird peeking out. Clearly signals "chat" + "song."

E. Flying (mid-air)

Bird caught mid-flight, wings flapping actively. Energetic, draws attention.
